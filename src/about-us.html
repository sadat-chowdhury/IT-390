About Streamlog

Streamlog is your go-to platform for tracking and rating movies and shows from all the most popular streaming services. Our goal is to deliver a service with seamless experience for users to log their favorite content and discover new recommendations.

The idea came from Andrew, our product owner, to create a web app that allows users to get recommendations based on their most loved shows, and movies from different streaming platforms.

Our team consists of 4 individuals in their last year of their undergraduate studies. Each member has been exposed to many web application development technologies. Database, Cloud, Software & Web Programming to name a few.
